The Waterford Lodge Hotel


About the Waterford Lodge


The Waterford Lodge really is a great location, moments from the centre of town, and adjacent to the award winning Carlisle Park, an ideal location for wedding photography. This beautiful 18th century coaching inn has masses of character and charm. The Waterford Lodge Hotel is the perfect venue to celebrate your special occasion. Situated in the delightful market town of Morpeth, the Waterford Lodge really is a great location, moments from the centre of town. The Waterford Lodge has splendid accommodation, including our luxurious bridal suite with four poster bed. A choice of double, twin and family rooms is available, all fitted with en-suite facilities. It also has a fantastic restaurant and bar as well as a large and spacious function room.

Our Function room


Whether it's for a wedding, birthday, or any other celebration - the Waterford lodge is the perfect place.
With the capacity for up to 150 guestsour beautiful function room has everything you will need to make your day a truly special one. The functions area offers a private bar and a large outdoors area that you can hire as well (for an additional cost).
We can cater for any event; our kitchen also works very hard to make your occasion memorable, whether you'd prefer a three course meal or a buffet, our chefs will make sure everything is perfect for you. We offer various drinks packages, allowing you to choose the perfect beverages to suit your occasion.
