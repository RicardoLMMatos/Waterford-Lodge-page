The Waterford Lodge Hotel


Welcome to the Waterford Lodge

A Timeless 18th Century Retreat in Morpeth


Step into the Waterford Lodge, a beautifully preserved 18th-century coaching inn that exudes charm and character. Situated in the heart of Morpeth, this historic gem offers a perfect blend of tradition and modern comfort. Located just a stone's throw from the vibrant town center and the award-winning Carlisle Park, it's an idyllic setting for any occasion, including picture-perfect wedding photography.


Our pub is designed to cater to every guest's needs, whether you're here to enjoy a handcrafted cocktail, savor a pint of expertly poured draught beer, or cheer on your favorite team during a big match. With a diverse drinks menu that includes a wide range of beers, creative cocktails, and premium spirits, you're sure to find your favorite tipple in a welcoming and stylish atmosphere. The Waterford Lodge also offers elegant accommodation for those looking to extend their stay. Our luxurious bridal suite, featuring a romantic four-poster bed, is perfect for newlyweds. We also provide a choice of double, twin, and family rooms, all thoughtfully designed with en-suite facilities to ensure a comfortable and memorable visit.

Our Function room


Whether it's for a wedding, birthday, or any other celebration - the Waterford lodge is the perfect place.
With the capacity for up to 150 guestsour beautiful function room has everything you will need to make your day a truly special one. The functions area offers a private bar and a large outdoors area that you can hire too (for an additional cost).
We can cater for any event; our kitchen also works very hard to make your occasion memorable, whether you'd prefer a three course meal or a buffet, our chefs will make sure everything is perfect for you.
