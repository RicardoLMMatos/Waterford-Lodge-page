The Sunday carvery


Sunday Carvery


At the Waterford lodge Sunday carvery is a highlight of the week; we believe that we serve a delicious Sunday roast, with all our ingredients being fresh prepared and locally sourced from our massive fluffy Yorkshire puddings to our pork, sage and onion stuffing.
We use seasonal vegetables whitch means that you'll always get the best produce at the right time of the year.

We know that sometimes you fancy something else apart from a Sunday roast so we have something for everyone - even if you don't fancy a carvery, we still have a Sunday menu available.
