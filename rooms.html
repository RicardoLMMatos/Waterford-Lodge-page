Accommodation


Our rooms


In the centre of Morpeth, and with good transport links to Newcastle the Waterford has a convenient location, offering seventeen rooms, single, double, family rooms and even a bridal suite and, all of the rooms offer un-suite bathrooms, tea and coffe making facilities and tv.


To start your day we offer freshly cooked full English breakfast or continental breakfast options and we serve lunch and dinner Monday to Saturday with on Sundays we have our great carvery.


If you are a dog lover like we are, some of our rooms are also dog friendly so you can bring your pets with you on your travels

(for and exra £15 per night for the first dog and an extra £5 per night for any aditional dog).
